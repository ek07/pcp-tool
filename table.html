Dimension Reordering for Parallel Coordinates

Load data (.csv):

  • The first column needs to contain the class.
  • Computing time increases factorially with larger number of dimensions.
  • Data with more than 8 dimensions are split into partitions and won't provide results for all orderings.
  • 9 dimension: 2 partitions, 10-11: 3 partitions, 12+: 4 partitions
  • Higher QFD means the average distance of each PCP from each other is higher. i.e. they are more different.
  • Higher correlation => better PCP.
  • Smaller polyline distance => better PCP.
